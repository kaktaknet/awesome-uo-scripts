How to Install:-

Add Tevents=e_ep to all ur monsters so here is how ur monster will look
like

[CHARDEF 0900]

//ALIGNMENT=EVIL

CATEGORY=Monsters
SUBSECTION=Undeads

ITEM={ i_bone_chest 1 i_bone_leggings 1 i_bone_arms 1 i_bone_gloves 1 i_bone_helmet 1 0 5 }
