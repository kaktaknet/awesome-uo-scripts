_____________________________

v0.2 fixes and improvements
____________________________



-Added the possibility to customize the syntax highlighter through the syntax.highlighting file.
-Added a wider range of possibilities in customizing the syntax highlighter.
-Added the possibility to have a list of preset hosts to connect to (see hosts.ini).
-Added the possibility to save to log files what appears in the console.
-Added the possibility to disable/enable logging and if enabled, have it switch files daily or not.
-Added support for multiple languages.
-Added options to customize the language settings and the logging settings.

-Improved the program's layout management.  The console is now resizable at will.

-Fixed a bug which would leave the program on it's connected state even when connection was lost.
