!!Class Attribute Information!!
First off, each Attribute has thier own effect to your PvP and/stats in different ways. You will only have a Limited(30) amount of Stat Points to distribute. So make sure you distribute them according to your style of PvP you prefer. The HIGHER you the level of the Attribute, the more EFFECTIVE the corresponding factor will be. Here are the effects of each Attribute:
______
Power: This Attribute is for how much BONUS damage your opponent will take when hit. The higher this Attribute is, the more BONUS damage you will take from your victim.
______
Technique: This Attribute is for how much BONUS stamina your victim will lose when hit. It is also for how many Abilities you can have activated at once(up to 5). The higher this Attribute is, the more stamina your victim will lose when hit, and the more Abilities you can have activated at once.
______
Endurance: This Attribute is for how much you will RESIST your opponents Power & Technique Attribute. The higher this Attribute is, the LESS EFFECTIVE your opponents Power & Technique is against you.
______
Discipline: This Attribute is for how Strong and Effective your Special Spells are. The higher this Attribute is, the more Powerful and Effective your Special Spells will be.
______
Wisdom: This Attribute is for what level of your Special Spells your are able to cast. The higher this Attribute is, the Higher Level Special Spells you can cast.  NOTE: If you wish to cast ALL of your Special Spells, this MUST be Maxed.
Tip: Each Attribute plays a major part in your PvP. No Attribute is useless, even if one is maxed out, it will make a WORLD OF DIFFERENCE to your Survival/Kill rate!
